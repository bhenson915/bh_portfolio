Case Study

Fish Creek Animal Hospital

An animal hospital located in Mansfield, Texas that treats pets of all varieties. Fish Creek was looking for a new web presence to grow their customer base.

View Website

Date

Fall 2017 - Spring 2018 (2 semesters) Communication Technology 3320 and 4323

Domain

  • Travel
  • Culture

Context

At a Glance

This was an individual based project in UTA's Communication Technology course 3350, Web Design and Development 2, where we explored concepts like Block Element Modifiers Embark grew out of an interaction design assignment from UTA's Communication Technology course, Multimodal Design course, then completed in the User Experience course. Our prompt called for an intelligent system that increases awareness and cultural exploration that would meet the needs of target users at a specific location. We arrived at our final concept through contextual research, iterative research and prototyping.

Our final deliverable for the first semester was a brand and style guide, a working prototype, and a promotional video. For the second semester our final deliverable was a full UX research workup (user personas, user journeys, Axure prototyping, development, user testing, and QA/QC).
