Case Study

EMBARK Travel Services

A travel service company focused on different aspects of cultural tourism. Customers can experience art, language, music, and cuisine from around the world while either traveling abroad or from home via subscription boxes.

Context

At a Glance

Embark grew out of an interaction design assignment from UTA's Communication Technology course, Multimodal Design course, then completed in the User Experience course. Our prompt called for an intelligent system that increases awareness and cultural exploration that would meet the needs of target users at a specific location. We arrived at our final concept through contextual research, iterative research and prototyping.

Our final deliverable for the first semester was a brand and style guide, a working prototype, and a promotional video. For the second semester our final deliverable was a full UX research workup (user personas, user journeys, Axure prototyping, development, user testing, and QA/QC).

Our initial exercise was to explore the abstract concept of Cultural Tourism through the use of some theoretical practices.

Signifiers

Signifier
The signifier is the pointing finger, the word, the sound-image. A word is simply a jumble of letters. The pointing finger is not the star. It is in the interpretation of the signifier that meaning is created.

Signified
The signified is the concept, the meaning, the thing indicated by the signifier. It need not be a 'real object' but is some referent to which the signifier refers. The thing signified is created in the perceiver and is internal to them. Whilst we share concepts, we do so via signifiers. Whilst the signifier is more stable, the signified varies between people and contexts. The signified does stabilize with habit, as the signifier cues thoughts and images.

Meronymy

The semantic relationship between a meronym, or a part of something, and its whole

Word Association

A path connecting the resources or two similar paths in which the resources are involved.

Moodboard

A type of collage consisting of images, text, and samples of objects in a composition. It can be based upon a set topic or can be any material chosen at random
